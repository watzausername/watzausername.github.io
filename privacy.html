Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Data Collection

75 Challenge does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers. All challenge data is stored locally on your device. Period.

Progress Photos

Progress photos remain on your device and are never uploaded, analyzed, or shared by the app. Your photos are yours alone.

Analytics & Tracking

The app does not use third-party analytics, tracking SDKs, or any telemetry services. We don't know what you're doing in the app, and that's by design.

Third Parties

75 Challenge does not sell, rent, share, or otherwise disclose user data with third parties. There's nothing to share because we don't collect anything.

Permissions

The app requests camera and photo library access solely for progress photo functionality. These permissions are optional and only used when you choose to take or select photos.

Contact
