Common Questions

Missed a day? The challenge resets by design. That's the whole point.

Data syncing? All data is stored locally on your device. No cloud, no accounts.

New phone? Transfer your data using iOS device backup and restore.

Lost progress? Ensure you're backing up your device regularly through iCloud or iTunes.
